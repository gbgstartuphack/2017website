How it works

The 111 participants will get 10 hours to create the best product/service. They work in teams (max five people) and can build whatever they want as long as it can stand on its own. We really want to encourage people to make something out of that idea they’ve been saving for later, rather than building something related to their job or studies. Of course, the most important part is that it’s fun and inspiring.

This is the third edition of the Gothenburg Startup Hack, successful Startup Hacks have been held in Gothenburg and Stockholm for a few years.

Why become a partner?

Connect your brand with the best minds in the Gothenburg tech community

Supporting Gothenburg Startup Hack will put your company in front of students, entrepreneurs, designers, hackers and startups that are eager to connect with the latest opportunities and trends. Partners to the event will inevitably increase their exposure to the digital and creative community. Connect with us.

Reach the community

During the events we will reach directly and indirectly about 130 000 people! Enthusiastic #GBGTECH community reaches several thousands on twitter!

Identify and recruit talent

Gothenburg Startup Hack is a great place to reach out to a wide audience of people from the local community in Gothenburg. It’s the perfect setup to find new, innovative talent in an environment where they actually demonstrate live how good they are at creating value.

Support a good cause

Connect with startups, hackers, makers and creators in the digital space as a supporter of Gothenburg’s tech initiatives and play an active role in promoting the local tech community.
